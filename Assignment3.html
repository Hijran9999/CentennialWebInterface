Dodge Durango

Home Page

The Dodge Durango is a mid-size SUV produced by Dodge, first introduced in 1998. Known for its muscular design and strong performance, it's available with V6 and V8 engine options, including the powerful 6.4L HEMI V8 in the SRT models. Recent models offer three-row seating, advanced Uconnect infotainment systems, and features like adaptive cruise control and lane-keeping assist. The Durango blends towing capability (up to 8,700 lbs) with modern tech and comfort, making it a versatile option for families and performance enthusiasts.

  • Engine options: 3.6L V6, 5.7L V8 HEMI, and 6.4L V8 in SRT models
  • Towing capacity: Up to 8,700 lbs (with V8 engine)
  • Seating: Three-row seating for up to 7 passengers
  • Infotainment: Uconnect system with available 10.1-inch touchscreen
  • Safety features: Adaptive cruise control, lane-keeping assist, forward collision warning
  • Performance: All-wheel drive (AWD) available, with sporty handling
  • Cargo space: Up to 85.1 cubic feet with seats folded
