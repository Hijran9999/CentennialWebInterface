Mor ve Ötesi

One of the most iconic alternative rock bands in Turkey, formed in 1995, known for their socially conscious lyrics and energetic performances.

Band Members

  • Harun Tekin - Vocals, Guitar
  • Kerem Kabadayı - Drums
  • Burak Güven - Bass
  • Kerem Özyeğen - Guitar

Favorite Albums

  1. Gül Kendine (1999) - A breakthrough album that captured a wide audience with its alternative rock sound and poetic lyrics.
  2. Dünya Yalan Söylüyor (2004) - The most successful album, featuring their hit song "Bir Derdim Var," which became a modern anthem in Turkey.
  3. Masumiyetin Ziyan Olmaz (2010) - A more mature album, showing the band's evolution with deeper lyrics and more complex compositions.
